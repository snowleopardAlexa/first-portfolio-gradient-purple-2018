Welcome to White Plains
Christmas Market!

Mark your Calendar! White Plains WinterFest Hpliday Market will be here before you know it! WinterFest offers a unique holiday shopping experience by showcasing artisan crafts and food vendors. Now in its third year, WinterFest has been modeled after the outdoor holiday markets that occur in many European cities during the holiday season. The City, in partnership with Brenidson Markets, is working to grow this event each year with more artisam vendors, entertainment and programming. We set up a large tent plus a number of chalets for vendors to offer interesting, hand-made gifts in a festive, holiday environment.

Are you an aspiring artist?

Each year the event showcases the local talent that flourishes in our area. Past yearrs have featured musical and dance groups from in and around White Plains. If your group would like to be part of the Winterfest line-up, please contact us via form below.
